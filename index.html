What is AlphaRoom?

AlphaRoom is a professional algorithmic trading environment where traders, developers, and quant analysts collaborate to design, test, and deploy automated trading strategies. Decisions are driven by data, logic, and probability — not emotions.

Core Concept

Rule-Based Logic

Every trade follows predefined rules including entry, exit, stop-loss, and target — no guesswork.

Automation

Strategies execute automatically with fast, precise order placement and zero emotional bias.

Data & Backtesting

Historical data is used to validate strategies and measure risk before live deployment.

AlphaRoom Features

Algo Trading Bots

Intraday, positional, hedging, and option-selling bots with fixed risk management.

Risk Management

Max daily loss, capital allocation rules, auto square-off, and kill switch systems.

Professional Setup

Advanced charts, order flow, open interest, and global market tracking.

AlphaRoom Vision

Our vision is to create disciplined, emotion-free traders who focus on consistency, long-term survival, and data-driven growth rather than gambling.
